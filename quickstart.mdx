This guide walks you through the essential steps to go from a fresh install to watching and participating in Twitch chat with full 7TV cosmetics support.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download and install Chatterino7">
    Download the latest stable release for your platform from the [installation guide](/installation).

    <Tip>
      If you already have Chatterino 2 installed, Chatterino7 is a drop-in replacement. Your existing settings and layout will be preserved.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Add your Twitch account">
    1. Open **Settings** (gear icon in the top-right, or press `Ctrl+P` and select Settings).
    2. Go to the **Accounts** tab.
    3. Click **Add account** and follow the OAuth flow in your browser to authorize Chatterino7.
    4. Once authorized, your account will appear in the list and be set as active automatically.

    <Note>
      Chatterino7 uses Twitch's official OAuth flow. Your password is never entered into the app.
    </Note>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Open a channel">
    With a Twitch account connected, you can open any channel's chat:

    1. Click the **+** button on an empty split, or press `Ctrl+T` to create a new split.
    2. Type the channel name in the input box (e.g. `forsen`) and press **Enter**.
    3. The chat for that channel will load in the split.

    You can open multiple channels side-by-side by adding more splits. Use **View > Split > Add split** or drag to resize the layout.
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set up 7TV cosmetics">
    7TV cosmetics — Name Paints, Personal Emotes, and animated avatars — are enabled automatically when your Twitch account is linked to a 7TV account.

    - **Paints** appear immediately on any user who has one active on 7TV.
    - **Personal Emotes** from other users appear in chat once their emote set is loaded.
    - **Animated Avatars** are shown in usercards when you click a username.

    No additional configuration is required. Cosmetics are fetched in real time via the 7TV EventAPI as you open channels.

    <Tip>
      To disable specific cosmetic effects, go to **Settings > General** and look for the 7TV section. You can toggle animated badges and paint drop shadows independently.
    </Tip>
  </Step>

  <Step title="Customize your layout">
    Chatterino7 supports a flexible multi-split layout:

    - **Resize splits**: Drag the dividers between splits to adjust their size.
    - **Tabs**: Group multiple splits into a tab using **View > New Tab**.
    - **Popout chat**: Right-click a split header and choose **Popout** to float it in a separate window.
    - **Themes**: Go to **Settings > Appearance** to switch between dark, light, and system themes.
    - **Font size**: Adjust the chat font size in **Settings > Appearance > Font**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
